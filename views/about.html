About the project

Built to keep campus life from getting lost in your inbox

The Smart Campus Event Planner brings every workshop, club activity, academic session, and networking event into a single, searchable place, so students never miss out and organizers spend less time chasing registrations by hand.

2026 Course project
SOEN 287 Concordia University
4 Team members
?

The problem

Event info is scattered across emails, posters, social media, Moodle, and group chats, so students miss things that matter.

!

Our approach

One central hub where students browse, register, and track events, while organizers create and manage them with real-time capacity tracking.

✓

The outcome

Higher event turnout, simpler registration management, and a clear picture of participation across the whole campus.

What you can do

Designed for two kinds of users

Students

  • Browse and search campus events
  • Register and cancel registrations
  • View a personalized dashboard
  • Track upcoming and attended events

Organizers & Admins

  • Create and manage events
  • Monitor capacity and registrations
  • Mark attendance for each event
  • View participation statistics
